Welcome to Doomed Collection. I’m a metalhead, obsessed with the haunting, soul-crushing essence of doom metal but never confined to a single subgenre.
I embrace it all - from the brain-ripping fury of brutal death metal to the sorrowful, epic weight of doom; from the raw, gloomy chaos of black metal to the triumphant, war-tinged anthems of power metal. My passion for metal knows no bounds - if it’s heavy, dark, or intense, it’s fair game, but I try to avoid anything that lacks true spirit like most of the metalcore/deathcore bands.
This isn’t some scholarly endeavor; it’s just a raw, unfiltered space where I share my thoughts, reviews, and discoveries from the deepest corners of the metal world.
Horns up, metalheads 🤘
